Smart Web Applications

Do you want to offer your customers and staff more than a brochure website where they can get useful work done? We specialise in producing smart web applications that improve your customer’s online experience, streamline and improve your day to day work and allow staff to get relevant information wherever they are.

Our applications integrate with your existing systems, take payment, work on mobile devices and are completely customised to your needs. We provide full support for your application after it is delivered and are always available to adapt and extend your applications as your business and market changes.

Go Mobile

The mobile phone you have in your pocket today is a mini computer with an array of sensors such as GPS, capable of communicating with other systems - your customers, potential customers and staff have similar devices as well.

We develop Apps for these devices that can make staff more effective out of the office, reach more customers and provide new innovative services. This includes native apps for Android Phones and Tablets, iPhones and iPads, and Blackberry, as well as producing mobile device optimised web applications.

Cloud Computing

Amazon, Google, sales force, vmware, rackspace and others offer computing on demand, where you only pay for what you use. As well as saving money, these services allow your systems to instantly “scale up” to cope with peaks in demand, to handle batch processing jobs or just cope with a growing successful business.

We develop our systems using a scalable web service approach that is ideally suited to take advantage of these services. To make sure your systems are cloud ready, get in touch with us now.
